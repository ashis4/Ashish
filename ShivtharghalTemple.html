Shivthar Ghal Temple

The Cave of Spiritual Solitude

Overview

Shivthar Ghal, also known as Sundarmath, is a tranquil cave temple associated with Saint Ramdas Swami, the guru of Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj. Nestled amidst the Sahyadri hills, this cave offers a serene retreat for meditation and reflection.

What To Expect?

The cave features a small shrine dedicated to Lord Shiva, with a stream of water flowing nearby. The walls of the cave have inscriptions and writings attributed to Ramdas Swami.

Tips

  • Wear waterproof footwear if visiting during monsoon.
  • Carry food and water, as facilities are minimal.
  • Explore the nearby waterfall for a refreshing experience.
  • Avoid loud conversations to maintain the sanctity of the place.
  • Ideal for meditation and quiet contemplation.
