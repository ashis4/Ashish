Mahalakshmi Temple

The Ancient Shrine of Prosperity

Overview

Mahalakshmi Temple, located in the village of Kelshi near Raigad, is a centuries-old temple dedicated to Goddess Mahalakshmi, the deity of wealth and prosperity. Known for its serene atmosphere and cultural significance, the temple is a popular destination for devotees and tourists alike.

What To Expect?

The temple features traditional Konkani-style architecture with intricate carvings and wooden pillars. Inside the sanctum, the idol of Goddess Mahalakshmi is adorned with jewelry and flowers, radiating divine energy.

Tips

  • Visit during Navratri for a grand celebration.
  • Combine your visit with a trip to the nearby Kelshi Beach.
  • Avoid littering in and around the temple premises.
  • Local markets near the temple offer traditional handicrafts.
  • Morning visits are recommended to enjoy the tranquil environment.
