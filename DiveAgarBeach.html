Diveagar Beach

A Peaceful Getaway with Golden Sands

Overview

Diveagar Beach is a serene and less-crowded beach located along the Konkan coast. Surrounded by coconut and beetle nut trees, it’s perfect for those seeking peace and tranquility.

What To Expect?

The beach is ideal for a leisurely stroll or relaxing by the water. It’s clean, with soft sands and a calm sea, making it safe for families. The nearby village offers a glimpse into local culture, and you can visit the Suvarna Ganesh temple for a spiritual touch.

Tips

  • Visit during weekdays for fewer crowds.
  • Carry snacks and water, as options are limited.
  • Avoid swimming during monsoons due to rough tides.
  • Enjoy sunset photography.
  • Guides are available for detailed history.Try local Konkani cuisine at nearby eateries.
