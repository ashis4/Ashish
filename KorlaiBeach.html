Korlai Beach

A Blend of Nature and Portuguese Heritage

Overview

Korlai Beach is a beautiful coastal spot that combines natural beauty with Portuguese heritage. Itâ€™s a perfect blend of beach, history, and culture.

What To Expect?

The beach is clean and quiet, with a mix of sandy shores and rocky areas. The Korlai Fort, a relic of Portuguese influence, is a major attraction here. Visitors can enjoy both the scenic beauty of the Arabian Sea and explore the historic fort.

Tips

  • Explore the Korlai Fort to learn about its history.
  • Wear sturdy shoes for walking around the fort and rocky areas.
  • Bring sunscreen, a hat, and sunglasses for protection from the sun.
  • Carry your own water and snacks as facilities are limited.
  • Great for a peaceful day with a mix of nature and history.
