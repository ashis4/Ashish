Overview

Kothaligad Fort, also known as Peth Fort, is a small fort near Karjat offering a relatively easy trek for beginners. The pinnacle of the fort resembles a chimney.

What To Expect?

The trek passes through a scenic village and dense forests. The fort has a cave with carvings and a water tank. The view from the top is stunning, with lush greenery and nearby forts visible on a clear day.

Tips

  • Ideal for a one-day trek.
  • Visit during the monsoon for lush greenery.
  • Wear proper trekking shoes.
  • Stay hydrated and bring snacks.
  • Avoid littering during the trek.
