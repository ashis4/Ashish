Featured Temples

Ballaleshwar Temple

Ballaleshwar temple is one of the eight “Ashtavinayak” temples of Lord Ganesha. It is located in the village of Pali which is at a distance of about 60 km from Karjat in the Raigad district.

Kanakeshwar Temple, Alibag

Kanakeshwar Temple is 10 km away from Alibag. It is situated on a small hill and is known for its cool climate and the old temple of Lord Shiva.

Shivtharghal

Shivthar Ghal, also known as Sundarmath, is a cave, about 34 km from Mahad, near Barasgaon, Maharashtra, India. Shivthar Ghal is located in Varandha Ghat in the Sahyadris on the Bhor-Mahad road.

Harihareshwar Temple

Harihareshwar is a town in Raigad district. The temple is said to be blessed by Lord Shiva and is significant for the Peshwa family.

Jagdishwar Temple

Its a perfect example of fantastic Architectural idea. The temple is built in such a way that the first rays of sun precisely fall on the Shiv Linga placed at the heart of the temple..

Shri Shirkaidevi Temple

Shree Shirkai Devi Temple is a hindu temple and tourist attraction located in Raigad, Maharashtra. The street address of this place is 6CPR+4HC, Gherakilla Raigad.

Shri Vireshwar Temple

This temple is dedicated to Lord Shiva and is located in a peaceful village setting. It is a popular destination for devotees.

Vyadeshwar Temple

Vyadeshwar Temple Raigad Fort is a hindu temple located in Raigad, Maharashtra. The average rating of this place is 4.80 out of 5 stars based on 29 reviews. The street address of this place is opposite Kushavart Lake, Raigad, Maharashtra 402305, India.

Shri Suvarna Ganesh Temple

While Murud Janjira is known for its fort, it also houses a small temple dedicated to Lord Ganesh, visited by tourists.
