Ballaleshwar Temple

The Divine Abode of Lord Ganesha

Overview

Located in Pali, Ballaleshwar Temple is one of the eight revered Ashtavinayak temples of Lord Ganesha in Maharashtra. It is the only temple among the Ashtavinayak where Lord Ganesha is worshipped under the name of a devotee, Ballal. The temple is known for its serene atmosphere and unique architecture.

What To Expect?

The temple is built in a traditional stone architecture style with intricate carvings. The idol of Lord Ganesha is made of pure stone and faces east, receiving the first rays of the morning sun. The templeâ€™s sanctum is designed to allow sunlight to fall directly on the idol during specific times of the day.

Tips

  • Visit early morning to avoid crowds.
  • Combine your visit with a trip to the nearby Sudhagad Fort.
  • Parking is available but can get crowded on festival days.
  • Carry cash for offerings, as digital payment options are limited.
  • Explore the nearby riverbanks for a serene experience.
