Bhivpuri Waterfall

The Adventurer’s Delight

Overview

Located near Karjat, Bhivpuri Waterfall is a favorite among adventure enthusiasts for activities like rappelling and trekking. The waterfall is easily accessible and becomes a hub of activity during the monsoon season.

What To Expect?

The cascading water falls from a moderate height, forming a shallow pool at the base, perfect for a refreshing dip. Visitors can enjoy the scenic trail leading to the waterfall, surrounded by greenery and small streams. Adventure lovers can opt for guided rappelling activities organized during the monsoon season.

Tips

  • Book adventure activities in advance for better planning.
  • Avoid visiting during weekends to escape the crowd.
  • Wear comfortable clothing suitable for water sports.
  • Carry an extra set of clothes and towels.
  • Visit nearby attractions like Karjat farms for a wholesome trip.
