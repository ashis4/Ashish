Harihareshwar Temple

The Dakshin Kashi of Maharashtra

Overview

Situated along the Konkan coastline, Harihareshwar Temple is a revered Hindu shrine dedicated to Lord Shiva. The temple is surrounded by four hills – Harihareshwar, Harshinachal, Bramhadri, and Pushpadri – adding to its natural beauty and spiritual significance.

What To Expect?

The temple complex is known for its intricate stone carvings and spiritual atmosphere. The adjoining beach is a major attraction, offering breathtaking views of the Arabian Sea.

Tips

  • Visit during early morning or late evening for the best views.
  • Explore the nearby beaches for relaxation.
  • Carry sunscreen and hats to protect against the sun.
  • Avoid visiting during high tide to access the Pradakshina path safely.
  • Sample local seafood at nearby restaurants.
