Raigad Fort

The Pride fo Maratha Empire

Overview

Raigad Fort, located in the Sahyadri Mountain range, served as the capital of the Maratha Empire under Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj. Known for its historical significance and breathtaking views, it remains a prominent tourist attraction.

What To Expect?

The fort is situated atop a steep hill and is accessible by trekking or via ropeway. Visitors can explore the royal palaces, ancient water reservoirs, and the Maha Darwaja, which stands as a symbol of Maratha pride. The Samadhi of Shivaji Maharaj and the Jagdishwar Temple are must-visit spots. The fort offers panoramic views of the surrounding valleys, making it a perfect spot for history enthusiasts and nature lovers alike.

Tips

  • Wear comfortable trekking shoes.
  • Visit during the monsoon for lush greenery.
  • Carry water and light snacks.
  • Start early to avoid the afternoon heat.
  • Guides are available for detailed history.
