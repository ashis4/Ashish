Murud-Janjira Fort

The Unconquered Island Fortress

Overview

Situated on an island near the coastal town of Murud, Janjira Fort is an architectural marvel and a symbol of the Siddis' naval supremacy. The fort remains unconquered despite several attempts by foreign powers.

What To Expect?

The fort is accessible via boat rides from the coast. You can explore its massive gates, freshwater tanks, and bastions equipped with ancient cannons. The fortâ€™s strategic location in the Arabian Sea offers scenic views and insights into its defensive design.

Tips

  • Check the boat service timings.
  • Visit during winter for calm seas.
  • Avoid visiting during monsoon due to rough waters.
  • Bring a hat or sunscreen for the sunny boat ride.
  • Hire a local guide to learn about the fort's history.
