Lingmala Waterfall

The Majestic Marvel of Mahabaleshwar

Overview

Lingmala Waterfall is one of the most popular attractions in Mahabaleshwar, located amidst the misty valleys of the Western Ghats. It is fed by the Venna River and falls from a height of 600 feet, creating a spectacular sight. The area is surrounded by lush greenery, making it a favorite spot for tourists and photographers.

What To Expect?

The waterfall is divided into two parts – the main waterfall, which is a steep plunge, and a smaller cascade that’s accessible for swimming. The viewpoint offers a panoramic view of the valley and the falls, especially mesmerizing during the monsoon. The walk to the waterfall is easy, with paved paths and shaded areas. You can also explore nearby attractions like the Venna Lake and Mapro Garden to make it a wholesome trip.

Tips

  • Avoid getting too close to the edge for safety.
  • Visit during monsoon for the best views but carry a raincoat.
  • Wear sturdy footwear for the slightly muddy trails.
  • Carry a camera for capturing the scenic beauty.
  • Explore nearby strawberry farms for a unique experience.
