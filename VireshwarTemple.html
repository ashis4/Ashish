Vireshwar Temple

The Sacred Seat of Lord Shiva

Overview

Located in Mahad, Vireshwar Temple is an ancient shrine dedicated to Lord Shiva. Known for its beautiful architecture and spiritual significance, the temple is a prominent pilgrimage site for devotees in Raigad.

What To Expect?

The temple features intricately carved stone walls, with the idol of Lord Shiva enshrined in the sanctum. The temple's serene surroundings make it an ideal spot for meditation and prayer.

Tips

  • Combine your visit with a trip to the Gandharpale Caves.
  • Carry sufficient water and snacks if visiting during summer.
  • Avoid visiting during peak monsoon due to slippery trails.
  • Explore local Mahad markets for souvenirs.
  • Early morning or evening visits are recommended for a tranquil experience.
