Murud Beach

A Coastal Retreat with Stunning Views

Overview

Murud Beach is a coastal retreat known for its stunning views and calm atmosphere. The beach offers the perfect blend of natural beauty and tranquility.

What To Expect?

The beach is surrounded by lush greenery and is ideal for long walks. Itâ€™s also known for its historical significance with the presence of the famous Murud Janjira Fort nearby. Visitors can relax by the serene waters or enjoy a boat ride to the fort.

Tips

  • Visit the Murud Janjira Fort for a glimpse into history.
  • Take a boat ride to the fort for the best views.
  • Carry sunscreen and a hat for sun protection.
  • Visit during weekdays for a less crowded experience.
  • Pack a picnic for a relaxing day at the beach.
