Mandle Waterfall

The Hidden Gem of Raigad

Overview

Mandle Waterfall, located in the Raigad district, is a lesser-known yet spectacular destination for those seeking tranquility and adventure. It is surrounded by dense forests and rolling hills, offering a serene escape from the city’s hustle. The waterfall comes alive during the monsoon season when the streams are at their fullest.

What To Expect?

The cascading water creates a natural pool at the base, ideal for visitors to enjoy a refreshing dip. The trail to the waterfall passes through lush greenery, making it a scenic trek. The sound of the water crashing onto the rocks, coupled with the chirping of birds, provides a peaceful ambiance. This spot is ideal for picnics and group outings, offering plenty of space to relax amidst nature. Due to its secluded location, the waterfall remains unspoiled and less crowded.

Tips

  • Carry enough water and snacks for the trek.
  • Wear trekking shoes as the path may be uneven.
  • Visit during the early monsoon to avoid heavy rains.
  • Take a guide if you’re unfamiliar with the area.
  • Pack a first-aid kit for emergencies.
