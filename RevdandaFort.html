Revdanda Fort

Coastal Ruins with Portuguese Charm

Overview

Revdanda Fort, located near Alibaug, is a lesser-known coastal fort offering serenity and historical significance. Its Portuguese origin is reflected in its architecture and design.

What To Expect?

The fort is partially in ruins but offers an old-world charm. Visitors can explore the watchtowers, bastions, and a church within the premises. The location by the beach provides a perfect blend of history and natural beauty.

Tips

  • Wear comfortable footwear for exploring.
  • Carry a camera for photography.
  • Plan a visit during low tide to explore the coastline .
  • Avoid visiting during high tide.
  • Pair your visit with other nearby attractions in Alibaug.
