Awas Beach

Untouched Beauty and Tranquility

Overview

Awas Beach is an untouched paradise located near Alibag. Known for its tranquility and natural beauty, it’s a perfect destination for those who want to escape the crowds.

What To Expect?

The beach offers a serene environment with soft golden sands and gentle waves. It’s perfect for a quiet getaway, whether you want to relax by the water or enjoy a peaceful walk along the shore.

Tips

  • Visit during weekdays to avoid crowds.
  • Bring your own food and water, as there are few options available.
  • Wear comfortable shoes for walking on the beach.
  • Carry sunscreen, a hat, and sunglasses for sun protection.
  • Ideal for long walks and a peaceful picnic by the sea.
