Mandwa Beach

Gateway to Scenic Coastal Adventures

Overview

Mandwa Beach is a popular coastal destination known for its scenic beauty and proximity to Mumbai. It's the gateway for travelers heading to other Konkan beaches.

What To Expect?

The beach is surrounded by lush greenery and clear waters, perfect for relaxation. Visitors can also enjoy a boat ride to nearby beaches and visit the nearby village for local experiences.

Tips

  • Catch a ferry from Mumbai to Mandwa for a scenic ride.
  • Enjoy the clear waters, but avoid swimming during the monsoon.
  • Carry water and snacks as food options are limited.
  • Explore the nearby coastal areas for a day trip.
  • Visit during the off-season for a less crowded experience.
