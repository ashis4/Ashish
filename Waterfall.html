Featured Waterfalls

Tamhini Waterfalls

Located near Tamhini Ghat, this waterfall is known for its beauty during the monsoon season. It’s a perfect spot for nature lovers and photographers, surrounded by lush greenery.

Mandle Waterfall

Mandle Waterfall is located in the Raigad district of Maharashtra, near the village of Mandle. It is one of the hidden gems in the region, especially popular during the monsoon season when the waterfall is at its fullest and most spectacular. The falls are surrounded by lush greenery, making it a great destination for nature lovers, trekkers, and photographers.

Lingmala Waterfalls

Lingmala Waterfall is a stunning two-tiered waterfall located near Mahabaleshwar. The cascading water is surrounded by a beautiful landscape, offering breathtaking views from various spots.

Chinaman’s Waterfall

Situated near Mahabaleshwar, Chinaman’s Waterfall is one of the most picturesque waterfalls in the region. It’s a great spot for nature walks and photography.

Bhivpuri Waterfall

Bhivpuri Waterfall, located near Karjat, is a popular trekking destination. The waterfall is a perfect escape for nature lovers, especially during the rainy season when the water flows in full force.

Devkund Waterfall

Devkund Waterfall is a stunning waterfall located near Bhivpuri, Karjat. Known for its clear blue water, it is a popular trekking destination, offering a beautiful view and a refreshing dip.

Sudhagad Waterfall

Sudhagad Waterfall is located near Sudhagad Fort and is known for its tranquil surroundings. It is a great spot for those who enjoy a peaceful hike amidst nature.

Kundalika Waterfall

Kundalika Waterfall, located near Kolad, is famous for white-water rafting. The waterfall is a popular destination for adventure enthusiasts and those seeking a thrilling experience.

Sondai Waterfall

Sondai Waterfall is located near Sondai Fort and offers a peaceful environment with scenic beauty. It’s an ideal destination for trekkers and nature lovers.
