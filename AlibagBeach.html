Alibag Beach

The Iconic Coastal Destination

Overview

Alibag Beach is a popular destination near Mumbai, known for its scenic beauty, historic forts, and vibrant atmosphere.

What To Expect?

The beach is bustling with activities, including water sports, horse rides, and ferry rides to Kolaba Fort. The golden sands and the view of the fort during sunset are highlights. Itâ€™s also a hub for food and shopping.

Tips

  • Plan your visit during the off-season to avoid crowds.
  • Try the local seafood delicacies.
  • Check the tide timings for a walk to Kolaba Fort.
  • Bring sunscreen and beachwear.
  • Avoid weekends for a quieter experience.
