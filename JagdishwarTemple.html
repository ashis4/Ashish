Jagdishwar Temple

The Royal Temple of Raigad Fort

Overview

Located at Raigad Fort, Jagdishwar Temple was built by Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj and remains a historical and spiritual landmark. The temple is dedicated to Lord Shiva and is a fine example of Maratha-era architecture.

What To Expect?

The temple features a simple yet elegant design, with stone walls and a serene courtyard. The idol of Lord Shiva is placed in the sanctum, and visitors can see the samadhi (tomb) of Shivaji Maharaj nearby.

Tips

  • Combine your visit with a trek to Raigad Fort.
  • Wear comfortable shoes for exploring the fort.
  • Hire a guide to learn about the historical significance of the temple.
  • Carry enough water and snacks for the trip.
  • Visit during early morning or evening to avoid the heat.
