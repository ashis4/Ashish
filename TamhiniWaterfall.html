Tamhini Waterfall

The Cascading Jewel of the Sahyadris

Overview

Located in the lush green Tamhini Ghat near Mulshi, Tamhini Waterfall is one of the most stunning waterfalls in Maharashtra. Nestled amidst the Western Ghats, the waterfall is especially mesmerizing during the monsoon season when the surrounding valleys are covered in mist and greenery. It is a popular spot for nature lovers, photographers, and trekkers.

What To Expect?

Visitors can enjoy the thunderous sound of water gushing down the rocky cliffs surrounded by dense foliage. The misty ambiance, chirping birds, and cool breeze create a serene experience. The area offers several small waterfalls and streams, perfect for relaxing or capturing scenic photos. The roads leading to Tamhini are equally picturesque, with lush hills, winding paths, and breathtaking viewpoints. You can also explore nearby attractions like Mulshi Dam and Tamhini Ghat viewpoints to make the most of your trip.

Tips

  • Wear comfortable shoes as the area can get slippery.
  • Carry extra clothes if you plan to take a dip in the water.
  • Avoid visiting during peak monsoon as roads may be unsafe.
  • Bring snacks and water, as food stalls are limited.
  • Best visited early morning to avoid crowds.
