Kankeshwar Temple

The Sacred Hilltop Temple

Overview

Perched atop a hill near Alibaug, Kankeshwar Temple is dedicated to Lord Shiva and is known for its scenic location and spiritual significance. Surrounded by dense forests, the temple offers panoramic views of the Konkan coastline and the Arabian Sea.

What To Expect?

The temple is reached via a 700-step climb through a picturesque trail lined with dense vegetation and small shrines. The main temple is an architectural marvel, featuring intricately carved stone sculptures and a tranquil atmosphere.

Tips

  • Start your climb early to avoid the afternoon heat.
  • Wear comfortable footwear for the ascent.
  • Carry water and snacks as there are limited facilities at the top.
  • Avoid visiting during heavy monsoons as the steps can get slippery.
