Sondai Waterfall

A Scenic Retreat Near Sondai Fort

Overview

Located at the base of Sondai Fort near Karjat, Sondai Waterfall is a seasonal gem that comes alive during the monsoon. The waterfall offers a picturesque setting with its cascading streams surrounded by verdant hills. It is a perfect spot for a day trip or picnic.

What To Expect?

The short trek to the waterfall is easy and suitable for beginners. Along the trail, youâ€™ll encounter small streams, rocky patches, and lush greenery. The waterfall itself is a gentle cascade forming a shallow pool, making it ideal for families and groups. The area is tranquil, offering visitors a chance to relax and enjoy nature. The nearby Sondai Fort is an additional attraction for history buffs and trekking enthusiasts.

Tips

  • Wear comfortable footwear as the trail may get muddy.
  • Carry snacks and water as there are no facilities nearby.
  • Plan a visit during the early monsoon to enjoy the best views.
  • Keep an eye out for the local wildlife.
  • Combine the visit with a trek to Sondai Fort for added adventure.
