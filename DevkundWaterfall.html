Devkund Waterfall

The Enchanting Emerald Pool

Overview

Nestled near Bhira village in Raigad district, Devkund Waterfall is a stunning hidden gem surrounded by the dense forests of the Sahyadri range. This waterfall cascades into a natural emerald-green pool, making it one of the most scenic and serene destinations for nature lovers. It is also a confluence of three streams, adding to its mystic beauty.

What To Expect?

The trek to Devkund Waterfall is a moderate 3-hour journey through dense forests, river streams, and lush greenery. The sound of the cascading water grows louder as you approach the falls, creating a sense of excitement. Once there, visitors are greeted with the sight of water gushing down a cliff into a tranquil pool. The pristine environment and the cool breeze make it an ideal spot for photography and relaxation. However, swimming in the pool is restricted during heavy rains for safety.

Tips

  • Start the trek early to avoid the afternoon heat.
  • Wear waterproof and sturdy shoes for crossing streams.
  • Carry a raincoat and waterproof bags during monsoon.
  • Avoid littering and respect the natural environment.
  • Local guides are available; hiring one is advisable for first-timers.
