Karnala Fort

Trek Through Nature to a Historic Pinnacle

Overview

Karnala Fort, located within the Karnala Bird Sanctuary, is a popular trekking destination near Mumbai. Known as the "Thumb of the Deccan," the fort offers stunning views and rich biodiversity

What To Expect?

The trek to the fort passes through dense forests, home to various bird species. The fort is relatively small, with remnants of water cisterns and walls. The climb is moderately challenging and rewards visitors with spectacular views of the surrounding hills and valleys.

Tips

  • Wear comfortable trekking shoes.
  • Carry binoculars for birdwatching.
  • Wear sturdy trekking shoes.
  • Bring sufficient water and snacks.
  • Visit post-monsoon for pleasant weather.
