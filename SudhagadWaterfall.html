Sudhagad Waterfall

The Hidden Wonder of Sudhagad Fort

Overview

Located near the historic Sudhagad Fort, Sudhagad Waterfall is a serene spot surrounded by dense forests and rolling hills. Its secluded location makes it a peaceful retreat for trekkers and nature enthusiasts. This seasonal waterfall is best visited during the monsoon when it is in full flow.

What To Expect?

The trek to Sudhagad Waterfall is an adventure in itself, taking you through thick forests, rocky patches, and small streams. The sound of cascading water adds to the charm as you approach the falls. The waterfall forms a shallow pool at the base, allowing visitors to enjoy a refreshing dip. The lush surroundings and the view of the fort in the backdrop make it an excellent spot for photography and picnics.

Tips

  • Combine the trek with a visit to Sudhagad Fort for a complete experience.
  • Wear sturdy shoes for the rocky trail.
  • Pack light snacks and plenty of water.
  • Visit early in the morning to enjoy the peaceful ambiance.
  • Be cautious during monsoon as the trail can be slippery.
