Korlai Fort

A Fusion of Cultures at the Coastal Fort

Overview

Korlai Fort, situated on a hill near Revdanda, is a historic fort built by the Portuguese in the 16th century. Overlooking the Arabian Sea, it is known for its strategic location and well-preserved architectural elements. The fort also features a lighthouse that guides ships along the Konkan coast.

What To Expect?

Visitors can explore its Portuguese inscriptions, remnants of the fortifications, and the scenic lighthouse. The trail to the fort is easy and takes you through a small village and lush greenery. At the top, youâ€™ll witness breathtaking views of the sea and the Revdanda Creek.

Tips

  • Best visited during winter or early mornings to avoid the heat.
  • Bring binoculars for a closer view of the sea and lighthouse.
  • Wear comfortable clothing and hats for sun protection.
  • Visit the Revdanda Beach nearby for a relaxing end to your trip.
  • Pack some snacks as food options are limited in the area.
