Harihareshwar Beach

A Spiritual Escape by the Arabian Sea

Overview

Known as the "Dakshin Kashi," Harihareshwar Beach is a spiritual destination surrounded by hills, beaches, and temples.

What To Expect?

The beach is peaceful and offers a mix of natural beauty and religious significance. The Harihareshwar temple is a major attraction, and you can also explore the nearby rocky shores and small caves. The scenic views of the Arabian Sea are mesmerizing.

Tips

  • Visit the temple early in the morning.
  • Wear sturdy footwear for exploring the rocky shoreline.
  • Carry your essentials as the area is less commercialized.
  • Try the local Konkani dishes.
  • Avoid swimming in rocky areas.
