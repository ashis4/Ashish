Nagaon Beach

Perfect for Water Sports and Relaxation

Overview

Nagaon Beach is a lively beach known for its water sports and pristine beauty. Itâ€™s a popular choice for adventure enthusiasts and those looking to unwind.

What To Expect?

The beach is perfect for water activities like parasailing, banana boat rides, and jet skiing. The soft sands and clear waters make it ideal for relaxation, too. It has a lively atmosphere with vendors and small eateries.

Tips

  • Try water sports like parasailing or jet skiing.
  • Visit early to avoid the crowd.
  • Carry sunscreen, a hat, and sunglasses.
  • Ensure safety while participating in water sports.
  • Eat at the local seafood joints for fresh dishes.
