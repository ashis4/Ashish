Kundalika Waterfall

The Adventurer’s Paradise

Overview

Situated near Kolad, Kundalika Waterfall is a popular destination for adventure enthusiasts and nature lovers. Known for its proximity to the Kundalika River, which is famous for white-water rafting, this waterfall offers a thrilling experience surrounded by lush greenery.

What To Expect?

The waterfall is most vibrant during the monsoon when the water gushes down the rocks with great force. Visitors can enjoy the refreshing spray of water and relax by the stream. The surrounding area is filled with lush vegetation, making it a great spot for picnics and nature walks. Adventure lovers can also explore nearby activities like rafting, kayaking, and ziplining at the Kundalika River. The combination of serene beauty and adrenaline-pumping activities makes it a must-visit destination.

Tips

  • Combine your visit with white-water rafting for a full-day adventure.
  • Avoid visiting during heavy rains due to strong currents.
  • Carry an extra pair of clothes if you plan to get wet.
  • Wear waterproof footwear for better grip.
  • Book adventure activities in advance during peak seasons.
